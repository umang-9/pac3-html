Bringing global healthcare to you

PAC3 is an EU-GDP licensed pharmaceutical supplier based in Malta, offering a broad spectrum of quality products and services to EU, MENA and Sub-Saharan Africa regions.

Since 2012, we’ve built a trusted international network of strategic partnerships to provide total healthcare solutions to our clients.

Our goal is proactive, dynamic service to assist our customers in ever-changing markets.

A global understanding of available products and regulatory requirements, coupled with a detail-orientated approach to our client’s needs means we bring we bring expertise to each sector of our op-erations.

From emergency supply services and the wholesale of pharmaceutical products, storage and logistics, regulatory compliance services, to market access and promotion, we deliver cost-effective, internation-al excellence.

Our client list includes clinics, hospitals, wholesalers, distributors, governments and their respective agencies. PAC3 is proud of our proven track record as a trusted partner in pharmaceutical supply.
